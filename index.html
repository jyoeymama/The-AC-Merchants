About Us

Welcome to The AC Merchants, the premier trading hub for Animal Company VR enthusiasts! We specialize in facilitating trades for both rare and common items within the game. Our community of dedicated traders works together to help everyone complete their collections and find their most desired items.

Whether you're looking for that ultra-rare legendary item or just want to exchange some common duplicates, our members are here to help make fair and exciting trades happen!

Trading Rules

  • No real-money transactions allowed - in-game items only
  • Be respectful to all members of the community
  • Do not spam trade requests
  • Report any scammers immediately to the moderators
  • Trade codes are valid for 24 hours unless otherwise specified
  • Always confirm receipt of items after a successful trade

Rarity Guide

Understanding item rarity is crucial for fair trading. Here's our color-coded system:

Legendary

Exceptionally rare items. Found in less than 1% of gameplay.

Epic

Very rare items. Found in approximately 3-5% of gameplay.

Rare

Uncommon items. Found in approximately 10% of gameplay.

Uncommon

Somewhat common items. Found in approximately 25% of gameplay.

Common

Easily found items. Found in more than 50% of gameplay.
